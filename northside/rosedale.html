Rosedale Avenue Apartments

One-bedroom and two-bedroom apartments

While Rosedale is one of the furthest options from Danforth Campus, its value and dedicated Gold Line bus stop make up for it. Although there has historically been some vehicle-related crime in the area, additional security patrols have been deployed recently.
