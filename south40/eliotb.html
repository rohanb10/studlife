Eliot B

Mostly four-person modern suites with singles and common rooms; some standalone (non-suite) doubles

Eliot B House is a hidden gem—it’s got all the convenience of South 40 House but less of the hype.

Pros

Attached to South 40 House, which houses Bear’s Den and the South 40 Fitness Center

Relatively new, well-furnished building

Convenient location in the middle of the South 40

Cons

Loading dock may be noisy for some suites

May be difficult for visitors to find

Price

Single: $11,938

Double: $10,670

Walk times:

Brookings: 15 min

DUC: 6 min

Delmar Loop: 21 min
