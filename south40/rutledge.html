Rutledge Hall

Six-person traditional suites with common rooms; mix of single and double rooms

Although Rutledge Hall is an older traditional dorm, it makes up for a lack of certain amenities and bedroom space with a large common room, balcony and lower cost. Its location is close to both a Circulator stop and Bear's Den, and the suite-style housing allows for close friendships to form between all six suitemates.
