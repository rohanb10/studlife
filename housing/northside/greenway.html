Greenway Apartments

One-bedroom, two-bedroom and three-bedroom apartments; three-bedroom townhouses

Greenway offers unique townhouse-style living for many of its three-bedroom units. Located between Danforth Campus and the Delmar Loop, these spacious apartments offer a great value for students looking to live off-campus.

Pros

Street and dedicated parking

Large and well-furnished

Unique townhouse layout for some three-bedroom apartments

Cons

No dishwashers

Non-townhouse apartments can feel like a dorm

Low social interaction in townhouses

Price

Single: $11,938

Walk times:

Brookings: 12 min

DUC: 12 min

Delmar Loop: 2 min
