Nemerov House

Mostly four-person modern suites with singles and common rooms; some standalone (non-suite) doubles

Nemerov House is a spacious modern dorm in close proximity to the parking garage, Student Health Services and the Circulator stop.

Pros

Large bedrooms and common rooms

Close to Circulator stop and Student Health Services

Large lobby and common areas with table tennis

Cons

Far from mailroom and the Swamp

No printers in building

Proximity to baseball/softball fields can be noisy

Price

Single: $11,938

Double: $10,670

Walk times:

Brookings: 14 min

DUC: 6 min

Delmar Loop: 21 min
