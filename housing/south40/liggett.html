Liggett House

Mostly four-person modern suites with singles and common rooms; some three-person suites and standalone (non-suite) doubles

Spacious and well-appointed, Liggett House is the only sophomore housing directly attached to a freshman dorm.

Pros

Close to Danforth Campus, parking and Clocktower

Spacious, modern lounge areas and study rooms

Large front yard

Cons

Far from the Swamp

Noise on south side due to loading dock

Only one kitchen for Liggett and Koenig

Price

Single: $11,938

Double: $10,670

Walking time to:

Brookings: 11 min

DUC: 4 min

Delmar Loop: 19 min
