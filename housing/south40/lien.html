Lien House

Mostly four-person modern suites with doubles; some non-suite doubles and triples

With comfortable rooms and common spaces, Lien House is a great place to both socialize and study. It is also conveniently located near the Underpass that leads to Danforth Campus.

Pros

Spacious bedrooms and floor common rooms

Close to main campus and Clocktower

Outdoor terrace on second floor

Cons

Very large floors

Far walk to mailroom and the Swamp

Tour guides may be a bother for lower floors

Price

Double: $10,670

Triple: $10,670

Walk times:

Brookings: 12 min

DUC: 5 min

Delmar Loop: 20 min
