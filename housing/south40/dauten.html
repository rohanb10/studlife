Dauten Hall

Six-person traditional suites with common rooms; mix of single and double rooms

Although Dauten Hall is traditional, it has elements that most moderns do not have, such as a full kitchen and large suite common rooms. It is located close to a Circulator stop and allows for tight friendships to form between suitemates.

Pros

Full kitchen in basement

Close to a Circulator stop

Fully furnished suite living with a spacious common room

Cons

Noisy due to traffic on Big Bend Boulevard

Low floor interaction

No elevator and narrow hallways makes for difficulty moving in
