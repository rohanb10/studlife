Umrath House

Mostly four-person modern suites with doubles; some standalone (non-suite) doubles and triples

Umrath House’s proximity to Bear’s Den and the Clocktower makes its location ideal, and its relatively new renovation makes it one of the most modern dorms on the South 40. With small floors, a fully equipped computer lab and a kitchen area, the only major drawback of living in Umrath seems to be the price.

Pros

One of the newest dorms

Extremely close to Bear’s Den

Bear Necessities in building
