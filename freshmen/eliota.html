Eliot A Hall

Mostly four-person modern suites; some suites have two doubles, some have four singles; some standalone (non-suite) doubles

Eliot A House is a great modern option with ample amenities across the building. Its central location is great, considering its close proximity to Bear’s Den, but it lies a bit further away from the Danforth Campus than most dorms.

Pros

Thirty-second walk to Bear’s Den and parking garage

Spacious lobby, library and kitchen

Patio with seating overlooking the Swamp

Cons

Tricky bike parking

No dedicated music practice rooms

Not connected to Eliot B House, which sometimes causes confusion

Price

Single: $11,938

Double: $10,670

Walk times:

Brookings: 16 min

DUC: 7 min

Delmar Loop: 22 min
