Danforth House

Mostly four-person modern suites with doubles; some standalone (non-suite) doubles; first floor has one three-person and one two-person suite with singles

Although Danforth is one of the farthest freshman dorm options, residents have particularly strong floor communities and enjoy the modern dorm luxuries. Many residents choose to stay within the Residential College sophomore year.

Pros

Floors tend to have strong communities

Computer lab, laundry room and classroom on first floor

Close to the Swamp

Cons

Two odd suites on first floor detached from neighbors

Far from Danforth Campus

Noise from the Swamp
