Shepley House

Mostly four-person modern suites with singles and common rooms; some standalone (non-suite) doubles; a few suites have two doubles instead of four singles

Shepley House is the furthest dorm from Danforth Campus, its nice units make it a good option for sophomores.

Pros

Close to the Swamp and basketball courts

Homey feel

Decently sized rooms and suites

Cons

Traffic on Big Bend Boulevard may be noisy

Far from main campus

May feel isolated

Price

Single: $11,938

Double: $10,670

Walk times:

Brookings: 17 min

DUC: 9 min

Delmar Loop: 24 min
